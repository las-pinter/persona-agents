# Skill: Ambiguity Resolution

## When to Use
Before producing any plan. Run through this checklist — do not plan around unknowns.

## Checklist

**About the goal:**
- [ ] What is the user/system trying to achieve?
- [ ] What does success look like? Is it measurable?
- [ ] What is explicitly out of scope?

**About constraints:**
- [ ] Are there deadlines or time constraints?
- [ ] Are there technology or platform constraints?
- [ ] Are there existing systems this must integrate with?

**About ownership:**
- [ ] Who is the end user of this feature?
- [ ] Who approves the output?
- [ ] Who is responsible for each affected system?

## Output
List every unresolved question explicitly in the plan's "Open questions" section. Do not proceed until critical ones are answered.
